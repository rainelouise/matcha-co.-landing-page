About Us

The Art of Perfect Matcha

Matcha & Co. started from a love for authentic Japanese matcha and a simple goal—to share that love with others. From home-brewed drinks to opening our café, we’ve grown by staying true to quality, creativity, and community.

We believe matcha is more than a drink—it’s a lifestyle. At Matcha & Co., we serve handcrafted beverages and treats made with premium ingredients, all in a cozy space where everyone can enjoy a taste of calm and comfort.

What Makes Us Stand Out

Quality Ingredients

We use only the finest quality matcha, sourced directly from Japan, ensuring that each product is pure, vibrant, and packed with flavor.

Expert Craftsmanship

Every matcha beverage and dessert is crafted by our skilled team, who have mastered the art of preparing matcha to perfection.

Inviting Atmosphere

Our cafe offers a cozy and inviting space where you can relax and enjoy your matcha experience in a welcoming environment.
